About me

Hello there! I'm Shane, a girly girl, lover of life and all things fashion. I’ve been all about the world of fasshion right from a very young age. You will find me either trying new clothes out, or going shoing for some cool outfits. I believe fashion is a way of life and affects everything about you. What you wear pretty much affect how people perceive you and so many other things. I’m not one to be confined in a particular fashion style. I love to explore all that there is. You could see me in a street stle today, and tomorrow find me looking all formal or victorian. what matters to me is that I’m comfortable and of course, look good. Who doesn’t wanna?

Despite the fact that I’ve always soaked myself in the world of fashion, I found myself studying Biology in College. Crazy right? But biology is also one field I love so much. It was my best subject in high school and still is. Getting to know how our bodies work has always fascinated me. What can I say, I had to know more.

Nonetheless being a fashion blogger is one of the best thing that’s ever haened to me. aart from the fact that I get to write about what I love, it does come with its own benefits. You will find that I’ve beeen feature on so many top magazines like Vogue, Elle, The Guardian and so much more. i also get to be the ambassodor of quite a number of to fashion companies like IMG Models, Beth Model Agency, House of Tara, Fashion Nova, and so much more. Having you guys subscibe tho this blog and seeing all your beautiful comments are also quite a big deal for me. I can’t thank you guys enough for choosing me. I must say, it’s been a super sweet ride so far and I’m glad I’m taking them with you guys.
